About

The story map library is derived from the storymap plugin for leaflet at http://atlefren.github.io/storymap/, and its functionality relies on both leaflet and Boostrap.

storymap.js | Released Date: 02/20/2017 | Version: 2.5 | MIT License

Author: Bo Zhao | College of Earth, Ocean, Atmospheric Science | Oregon State University

The context about wisconsin troutstreams was collected from Wikipedia, WDNR and jasparkatt.

The background video on the front page was clipped from Youtube.

The favicon image was acquired from my imagination.

This story map uses geospatial data from mapbox, Google Earth Engine, ESRI Map, Open Street Maps and WDNR and the ramblers fishing notes and faulty memories.
